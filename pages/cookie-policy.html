Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how MoneyTab.in uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics — for website traffic analysis. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — for displaying relevant advertisements. See Google Ads Policy.
  • YouTube — embedded videos on some pages may set cookies when played.
MoneyTab.in participates in the Google AdSense programme. Google may use cookies such as the DART cookie to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting Google Ads Settings.

How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.
  • Our cookie banner: When you first visit MoneyTab.in, you are shown a cookie consent banner. You can choose to “Accept All” or “Reject All” non-essential cookies.
  • Google opt-out: You can opt out of Google’s personalised advertising at adssettings.google.com.

Your Consent

By clicking “Accept All” on our cookie banner, you consent to our use of all cookies described in this policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
