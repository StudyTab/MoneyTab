Editorial Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MoneyTab.in is committed to publishing accurate, independent, and genuinely useful financial information for residents of Cuttack and Bhubaneswar, Odisha. This page explains how we create, verify, and update our content.

Our Editorial Mission

Our mission is to provide hyper-local, verified financial guidance that helps people in Odisha make informed decisions about loans, investments, government schemes, and everyday financial tasks. We cover topics that large national financial websites overlook — the specific rates at your local branch, the actual address of the EPFO office, the real process for applying to a local scheme.

Independence & Objectivity

MoneyTab.in is not funded by, affiliated with, or owned by any bank, NBFC, insurance company, or financial product issuer. We earn revenue exclusively through display advertising (Google AdSense). We do not accept:

  • Sponsored articles or paid placements disguised as editorial content
  • Payment from lenders to feature them positively in our guides
  • Affiliate commissions for recommending specific financial products
  • Press releases published as-is without independent verification

If we ever introduce affiliate links or sponsored content in future, we will clearly label them as such.

How We Research and Verify

Every guide published on MoneyTab.in is based on direct, first-hand research:

  • Branch visits: We personally visit or call the branches, NBFCs, and government offices mentioned in our guides to confirm addresses, phone numbers, operating hours, and rates.
  • Rate verification: Interest rates, fees, and scheme benefits are checked directly with the source — not sourced from third-party aggregators alone.
  • Date stamping: Every guide carries a “Last Updated” date. We update guides when we become aware of significant rate changes or policy updates.
  • No fabricated data: We do not invent rates, addresses, or testimonials. If we are uncertain about a figure, we clearly state that it is approximate or should be confirmed at the branch.

Corrections Policy

We take accuracy seriously. If we publish incorrect information, we correct it promptly and transparently. If you spot an error in any of our guides, please contact us with details. We will investigate and correct the information within 48 hours if the error is confirmed.

Not Financial Advice

All content on MoneyTab.in is for informational and educational purposes only. It does not constitute professional financial, legal, or tax advice. We recommend consulting a certified financial advisor, your bank, or the relevant government department before acting on any information published here. See our full Disclaimer.
