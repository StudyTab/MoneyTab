About MoneyTab.in

Your dedicated local finance resource for Cuttack & Bhubaneswar, Odisha.

Rakesh Rout
Founder & Finance Researcher

Welcome to
MoneyTab.in

Hi, I’m Rakesh Rout, the founder of MoneyTab.in. I started this site because I couldn’t find reliable, neighbourhood-level financial information for people living and working in Cuttack and Bhubaneswar.

When I needed a gold loan, I had to call five different branches to get the actual rate. When I wanted to start a SIP, I couldn’t find a verified list of local AMFI-registered distributors. MoneyTab.in was built to solve exactly this problem — one verified guide at a time.

I personally visit local banks, NBFCs, and government offices to verify every rate, address, and procedure published on this site. Nothing is copied from press releases or bank websites without a field visit to confirm it’s still current.

MoneyTab.in is not backed by any lender or financial institution. We earn through display advertising only. Our editorial decisions are 100% independent.

Please note: MoneyTab.in is an informational platform, not a financial advisory firm. Always consult a certified financial professional before making financial decisions.
